Hi Name,

Thank you for registering and consenting to the C-19 Human Genetics Study on COVID-19 susceptibility, conducted as a collaboration study between the University of Hawai’i and LifeDNA. You are helping with the global effort to better understand this devastating disease.

Please download the COVID Symptom Study App onto your smart phone or tablet from the Apple App Store or Google Play.

You can learn more here:

https://covid.joinzoe.com/us

When You Register To Use The App:

  • Answer for the first question: ”I am in an existing research study or trial, and I want my data to be shared with investigators on that study.” Yes, I am
  • Make sure you use the same e-mail address, name (first/last), and zip or postal code that you used for registering to the C-19 Human Genetics Study on the LifeDNA website. This is necessary for being part of the overall study.
  • Please indicate your cohort study membership “Is your study one of the following?”: Choose (or add): C-19 Human Genetics Study Study contact: Maarit Tiirikainen

How to use the App for the C-19 Study

To your best ability, please provide an answer for each question on the App even that most of them are optional. The more you provide, the more you will help both the C-19 Human Genetics Study and the COPE Study. The next times you provide an update there will be far less questions. We are hoping that you will provide daily updates on your symptoms, any COVID-19 testing, self-quarantining, management, and outcomes. If not daily, try to do this at least every 3 days or so, or anytime there is a change in you condition in any way. Allowing the App to send you notifications will be helpful. If you have not provided any updates for about a month, we will contact you via e-mail as a reminder to keep using the app. Mahalo.
