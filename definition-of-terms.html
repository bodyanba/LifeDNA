Definition of Terms

DNA
Deoxyribonucleic acid or DNA is a molecule made up of two polynucleotide chains that coil around one another to construct a double helix carrying genetic information for the development, function, growth and reproduction of all known organisms.
SNP
Single nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) is a specific site in your DNA at which more than one nucleotide/allele is found in the population.
