C-19 Human Genetics Study - Citizen Scientists Wanted

University of Hawaii and LifeDNA Research Collaboration Study on COVID-19

COVID-19 has upended daily life for citizens around the globe. Based on their genetics, certain individuals and populations may be impacted more severely. As the whole world has been following the spread of the virus, major differences in the SARS-CoV2 infection and the severe disease rates have been observed between the different geographic regions in the COVID-19 pandemic.

We are looking for participants who are between 18-60 years old and here is what we expect you to do:

Step 1

Register, read details about the study, and give your consent for your participation

Step 2

Use an App where you will provide your health data and report any symptoms every 1-3 days (App instructions will be provided in your confirmation)

Step 3

Provide a DNA sample for genotyping when requested
