COVID-19 Genetic Report
What does this test cover?
Our newly launched project is the first version of our COVID-19 report which contains well-studied, previously published genetic variants that are associated with the predisposition to coronavirus infection, the severity of the disease, and tolerance to known antiviral drugs. All genes and variants included in the report are based on the previous SARS, MERS, and other flu outbreak, and preliminary research available to date on the current COVID-19 outbreak.
