Age-related Macular Degeneration

Age-related macular degeneration (AMD) is an eye condition that can obscure the sharp, central vision you need for reading and other eyesight related activities.

Here are some information that can help you decide whether or not to view this report:
●
AMD is a common eye disease and the leading cause of vision loss for people aged 50 and older.
●
This disease is commonly found among people of caucasian descent.
●
This trait report does not cover all possible variants in the present genes or other genes associated with increased risk of Age-related macular degeneration (AMD)
