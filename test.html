Important Information About The COVID-19 Report:

●
The COVID-19 Report is made up of 3 reports: DNA, Lifestyle, and DNA + Lifestyle Report.
●
The COVID-19 DNA Report provides information on your risks for the coronavirus infection based on your DNA.
●
The COVID-19 Lifestyle Report shows your risks for the coronavirus infection based on your lifestyle, environment, personal habits, and certain important risk factors other than genetics. A questionnaire will be provided to you to generate your report.
●
The COVID-19 DNA + Lifestyle Report shows results based on the combination of your COVID-19 DNA and lifestyle reports.
●
It is possible that there are other genes associated with an increased risk of contracting COVID-19 that are not included in your report. Thus, you could still get infected even if there were no risk variants detected in your DNA.
●
The reports cannot guarantee if you will or will not get infected by disease.
●
The reports do not diagnose, treat, or cure COVID-19, nor does it offer any cures or solutions for the virus.
●
The COVID-19 DNA risk analysis is not a substitute for a COVID-19 diagnostic test. Consult your trusted healthcare professional for clinical diagnosis. Your genetic results should not be used to make medical decisions.

What You Can Do With the Results

If you have an increased risk for certain traits based on your report:
Utilize this report as a guide, bearing in mind that anyone can still contract COVID-19. Precautionary measures recommended by the WHO should still be followed.
Share your results with your family. They may also have these variants, and they may also be interested to learn about their risks.
If you are experiencing any symptoms of COVID-19, contact your doctor as early as possible to receive immediate medical attention.
Transform your lifestyle, improve your personal habits, and optimize your health in order to manage your non-genetic risks factors.
The COVID-19 Report is intended to provide you with both genetic and non-genetic information that can further help your consultation with a healthcare professional. This report should not be used to diagnose, treat, or cure COVID-19.

Important Information About The Disease Report:

●
The Disease report primarily provides information on an individual’s risk for specific diseases by identifying the presence of genetic variants, which are associated with an increased risk. This report does not diagnose, treat, or cure diseases, nor does it offer any cures or solutions for the diseases.
●
It is possible that there are other genes associated with your disease risks that are not included in your report. Thus, you could still be at risk even if there were no risk variants detected in your DNA.
●
This report cannot guarantee if you will or will not develop the disease.
●
This test is not a substitute for clinical diagnosis. If you think you are experiencing any symptoms related to the disorder, please consult your trusted healthcare professional. You may also opt to show your genetic results to them upon your discretion.
